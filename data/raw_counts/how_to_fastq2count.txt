1. Install enrich2.
2. Run: "enrich_cmd fastq2count.json counts wt --no-plots"
Note: you may need to trim and or reverse complement fastq files before count process.
To trim: trimmomatic SE read.fastq trim_read.fastq CROP:30
To reverse: seqkit seq -r -p -t DNA trim_read.fastq > reverse_trim_read.fastq

More details for Enrich2: 
https://github.com/FowlerLab/Enrich2
